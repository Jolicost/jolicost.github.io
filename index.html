Which accidents are serious or fatal?

Not every accident ends up being fatal or serious. Knowing the most important factors can be determining in order to prevent a major accident.

This visualization makes use of the data published by the department of transport of the United Kingdom. The data comphrends the accidents from the 2010 to 2014 period.

There was a total of 100000 accidents. Out of these, 50000 were considered major (they resulted in fatal or serious consequences).

This means that 33% of the accidents were considered major. This metric will be known as severity index from now on.

What can you do as a driver to prevent this kind of accidents? There are three crucial factors that influence the severity of an accident.

These three factors are the maneuver taken by the vehicle during the accident, the type of intersaction where the accident took place, and the point of impact of the vehicle.
